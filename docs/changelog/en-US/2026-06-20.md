# Update 2026.06.20

1. Characters now have a social life of their own: they meet each other automatically in the background (no manual trigger needed) and remember who they know — each other's identity, occupation, and the places they frequent. Even if you never open a particular character's chat, their life keeps moving.
2. When characters meet, their conversations have real substance: they talk about their relationship and shared memories, and about you (the deeper, more private things only when they trust each other enough — and only among your own characters, never leaking to anyone else). Conversation length feels more natural, and you can arrange a meeting right in chat ("go see her tomorrow") to make it actually happen.
3. Notifications upgraded to true push: you'll get proactive messages and LumeGram replies even after you've switched tabs or closed the browser. You can toggle each notification type, hide the content preview on your lock screen, and a message already delivered through an external channel (like Telegram) won't buzz you twice.
4. Improved character replies so they're more genuine: instead of restating their recent life in flowery prose, they actually say something new.
5. Fixed a relationship you set (like "old friend") being treated as a stranger, sending an image to a text-only model failing, and prompt times not being converted to your timezone.
