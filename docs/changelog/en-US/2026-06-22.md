# Update 2026.06.22

1. Characters remember what they actually did: ask "what did you do today?" and they can tell you the things they really got up to earlier, instead of drawing a blank — and the "remembered" mark on their schedule now only appears when a memory truly exists.
2. No more invented "we did this together" memories: when a character wants to do something with you, they now ask you in chat and wait for your yes before treating it as a shared memory; anything you didn't agree to is never recalled as if it happened — so a character can't insist on a date you never had and leave you second-guessing your own memory.
3. Story arcs you live through leave a mark: the key moments you go through together are remembered and carried forward in later conversation, with the big climactic ones becoming relationship milestones instead of vanishing without a trace.
4. Characters read the room better: in everyday small talk they no longer slip into a gushy, therapist-like tenderness (or repeat the same sweet phrasing over and over) — but when you genuinely open up about something hard, that warmth is still there.
5. More reliable push notifications: fixed some delivery issues and made notifications still arrive after a device wakes from sleep, so fewer get missed.
