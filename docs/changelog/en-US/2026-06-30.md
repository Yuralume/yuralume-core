# Update 2026.06.30

1. Self-hosting is simpler now: a single command installs the whole of Yuralume on your own machine, and updating is the same one line — your characters, memories, and conversations still stay entirely on your machine and nothing is sent back.
2. Fewer missed notifications: when you skip linking a chat channel, you're now reminded to turn on browser notifications, so busy follow-ups, reminders, and a character's own messages are easier to catch.
